Fortune Industries is a prestigious and innovative concept, designed and formulated by a team of business professionals headed by Mr. Issac Varghese a technocrat from the industry registered with registrar of companies. Started the company during the year 2014-2015 and started the commercial production only after having a specialized labour training about 6 Months. The unit is existing in 60 cents of land in KINFRA Industrial Area on the Coimbatore-Palakkad NH. The building is unique of its kind, providing safe working condition and ensuring protection from environmental hazards. The company having understood the increasing potential for ready to wear industry, company to be installing world class machines and equipment’s from best suppliers in India. We are the manufacturers, suppliers, exporters of formal, Casual and semi-casual shirts of our own brand out sourcing and design all kind of uniforms stitching (Industrial, Hospitals, Schools and Hotels etc.).Our product command good demand in Kerala, Tamil Nadu and Abroad. The factory is set up with Production Capacity of 156000 pieces per annum.

The initiative of the project is carried out by Mr.ISSAC VARGHESE., S/o. T.L.Varghese, Aged 43 years, residing at Thanikkal Residency, Hill View Nagar, Opp. ITI, Kanjikode (West) PO, Palakkad District who is the Chairman of the company. A business enthusiast and entrepreneur.

Mr. Issac Varghese was exposed to business from the age of 20 and has been associated with lot of Poineer ventures in Textile Industries. From 1999 to 2002 he was running a ready made shop at Kuwait known as Mama-Baba ready made. After his NRI life, he started a distribution company for supplying ready made garments in Palakkad. Over the years he has acquired good experience in textiles industries. He has developed contacts with retail and wholesale dealers and sure of achieving the new targets easily. He is to be textile business in foreign countries also which will be an added advantage in promoting the business.

Fortune Industries, is located on 60cents of prime industrial land with total project cost of Rs.2.33 crores. The building is unique of its kind, providing safe working conditions and ensuring protection from environmental hazards. The company having understood the increasing potential for ready to wear industry, company to be installing world class machines and equipments from best suppliers in India.

First of all there were both formal and casual shirts in Spanish V-Zac. Production of Spanish V-Zac was from Infant Jesus Factory located at Bangalore. The Consultation and Supervising was done by M/s. Sree Hari Sources, Bangalore. This was the most successful brand in the history of Fortune Industries. The production was from Bangalore. We have appointed new district distributors in each district and also we have exported the brand to three nations abroad.

Due to the perfectness in the stitching fabric quality and then we were getting more and more orders from many other sectors such as public limited companies situated in Kanjikode and also we were getting orders for uniforms from many schools in Palakkad district. Hereby the company decided to start the production in the factory which has to be constructed in the plot acquired in lease at Kinfra Textile Park. According to the marketing strategy of Spanish V-Zac shirts and the demand of need from other companies and institutions (uniform). We decided to set up a factory with with Production Capacity of 109200 Pcs per Annum.
